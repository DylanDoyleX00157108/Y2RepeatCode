Site Information

Welcome, movie fans, to Gorey Movie Reviews! This is a website for movie afficionados and critics or just those looking for a good movie to watch in our current situation. Movies of any genre or era are welcome, so long as your reviews are well written. Find fellow fans of a series or simply discover a new film to enjoy.

Consider yourself a film critc, an adoring fan or perhaps even an angry one? Why not write a review on a movie of your choice in our Write Review section? This can help you discuss the movies you love or help others find a movie to watch... avoid. Hopefully the former instead of the latter!
